Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cramberries, and Brie

wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cramberries, and creamy brie. It will became your new holidays starter!

image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Craberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup exstra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, thought it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/22 cups fresh or frozen cramberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough
    In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
